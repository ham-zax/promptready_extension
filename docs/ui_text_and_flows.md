## Copy Rules

- Use "successful BYOK AI cleanups" for the daily limit.
- Do not describe the limit as hosted free AI credits.
- Do not show legacy paid-flow, access-code, prelaunch, or prerelease copy in production.
- Do not claim everything stays local. Offline capture/export runs locally; BYOK AI cleanup sends captured content and the user's OpenRouter API key directly to OpenRouter.
