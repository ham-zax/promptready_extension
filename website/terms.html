PromptReady Terms

PromptReady provides free local capture, Markdown export, and JSON export for active-tab content.

Optional BYOK AI cleanup uses OpenRouter and is limited to 5 successful AI cleanups per local day. OpenRouter model costs, if any, are handled by your OpenRouter account.

Offline output remains available when AI cleanup is unavailable, fails, or does not meet PromptReady fidelity checks.
